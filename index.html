    Development Cheatsheet

    How To Play Roadside Realm

    Goal

    Find the Mapstone, defeat the Signpost Ogre, and return to the kiosk exit.

    Optional routes include the Forgotten Underpass, Never-Finished Mansion, Hidden Conservatory, and Soldotna Creek Wayside.

    Controls

    • Move: Arrow keys or WASD.
    • Inspect: Enter or Space.
    • Attack: F.
    • Use item: I.
    • Map: M.
    • Help: press Ctrl three times quickly.

    Combat

    Monsters block the tile ahead. Attack until they clear the path. Use healing items before HP runs out.

    When the Signpost Ogre winds up Big Spin, step backward to dodge it.

    Secrets

    Inspect suspicious walls, moon scratches, painted doors, wallpaper seams, and blue river routes.

    The main ending works without secrets. Extra routes unlock better endings and bonus rewards.
